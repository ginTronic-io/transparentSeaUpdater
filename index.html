How to enter DFU mode
  1. Hold the BOOT button on the back of your TransparentSea.
  2. While holding BOOT, press and release the RESET button (or connect the USB cable).
  3. Release BOOT. The device screen will go blank — it is now in DFU mode.
  4. Select a firmware version above, click Connect Device, and choose your device from the browser prompt.
  5. Click Flash Firmware and wait for the process to complete. Do not disconnect the USB cable during flashing.
